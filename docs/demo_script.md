# TrustTill Demo Script

## 90-Second Version

Opening:

"This is Mama Kemi. She sells food every day and receives transfers, but her business history is invisible to formal finance."

Show:

- Merchant profile
- 30-day sales
- evidence count
- credit-readiness score

Action:

"We paste a payment alert into TrustTill. The app structures the alert, adds it to the evidence trail, checks it for risk, and updates the merchant's readiness profile."

Paste:

```txt
CR 12500 from ADEWALE SUNDAY / lunch packs / 2:14 PM
```

Then click **Parse alert**.

Risk moment:

"If an alert looks suspicious, TrustTill does not hide it. It sends it to review."

Click **Risk sample**, then **Parse alert**.

Wema value:

"For Wema, this becomes a lightweight onboarding and credit-readiness layer. It helps identify promising micro-merchants, support safer onboarding, and build a pathway into digital banking."

Close:

"TrustTill does not ask informal merchants to become accountants. It makes their existing business evidence count."

## 3-Minute Version

0:00-0:25 Problem:

"Millions of small merchants receive money every day but remain invisible to formal finance because their business evidence is scattered across alerts, receipts, cash notes, and memory."

0:25-0:45 Solution:

"TrustTill turns those everyday traces into a clean merchant record: cashflow, fraud-risk signals, and credit readiness."

0:45-1:45 Live demo:

Show a merchant importing alerts, seeing categorized sales, getting a risk warning, then generating a credit-readiness profile.

1:45-2:20 Wema value:

"For Wema, this is an acquisition and lending-readiness layer. It helps identify promising micro-merchants, onboard them into digital banking, and support safer future credit."

2:20-2:50 Impact:

"For merchants, it means better records, safer payments, and a path from informal hustle to bankable business."

2:50-3:00 Close:

"We are not asking merchants to change behavior first. We are making the behavior they already have count."
